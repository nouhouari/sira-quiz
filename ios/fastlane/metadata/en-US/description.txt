Quiz Sîra is a free, fully offline educational quiz about the life of the Prophet Mohammed (SWS), drawn from authentic Islamic sources (Qur'an, Hadith, and classical Sîra literature).

FEATURES
• 70 carefully curated questions across ten thematic categories
  (Birth & Youth, Revelation, Meccan Period, Hijra, Medinan Period, Expeditions,
  Family & Companions, Character, Final Days, and the Qur'an & Message)
• Three difficulty levels — Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
• Progress tracking — you only see the questions you haven't mastered yet
• Two languages: English and French (switch any time in Settings)
• Light and dark mode
• Works fully offline — no internet connection required
• No ads, no analytics, no data collection of any kind
• No account or sign-in required

SOURCES
Every question shows its source reference (Qur'an, Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, or the Sîra of Ibn Hisham) so you can verify it and learn from it. The app is an educational aid; the content is respectful, suitable for all ages, and contains no figurative imagery of the Prophet (SWS) or the Companions.

PRIVACY
Quiz Sîra collects no personal data. All progress is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Ideal for students, educators, and anyone wishing to deepen their knowledge of the Prophet's life in a structured, engaging way.
