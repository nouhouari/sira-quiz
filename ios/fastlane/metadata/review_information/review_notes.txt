Quiz Sîra is a fully offline educational quiz app about the life of the Prophet Mohammed (peace be upon him).

No account, sign-in, or internet connection is required to use the app.
All quiz data is bundled and stored locally on device.
No ads, no analytics, no data collection.

The app contains text-based educational content only — no figurative imagery of the Prophet or the Companions.

Category: Education
Age rating: 4+
Primary language: English (French also available)
