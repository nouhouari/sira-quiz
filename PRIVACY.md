# Privacy Policy — Quiz Sîra

**Canonical (hosted) version:** https://nouhouari.github.io/sira-quiz/

_Last updated: 1 June 2026_

Quiz Sîra is an offline educational quiz about the life of the Prophet Mohammed (SWS).

**In short: the app collects no personal data and works entirely offline.**

- **No data collected** — no servers, no network calls that send your information anywhere.
- **On-device only** — quiz content and your progress (correctly-answered questions, language and
  theme preferences) are stored locally on your device and never leave it. Erase any time via
  *Settings → Reset progress*, or by uninstalling.
- **No tracking, ads, or analytics** — no advertising identifiers, no analytics/telemetry/crash SDKs,
  no third-party data-collecting SDKs.
- **No account or sign-in.**
- **No sensitive permissions** (no location, contacts, camera, microphone).
- **Children** — suitable for all ages; collects no data from anyone.

**Contact:** houarinourredine@gmail.com

The full bilingual (English / Français) policy is hosted at the URL above and is the version to enter
in the Google Play and App Store listings.
